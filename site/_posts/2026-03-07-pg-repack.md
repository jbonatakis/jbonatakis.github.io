## Setup
Insert 3M rows. Update half of them 6 times, then delete 97% of them.
```bash
INITIAL_ROWS=3000000 \
PAYLOAD_BYTES=1000 \
UPDATE_ROUNDS=6 \
UPDATE_MODULUS=2 \
DELETE_PERCENT=97 \
./scripts/run_bloat.sh
```
